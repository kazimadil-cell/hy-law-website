Our Story

Reliable, friendly and multilingual immigration advice

H&Y Law provides immigration advice and services to individuals navigating the complex UK immigration system, from our office at the Enterprise Centre, 50 Wakering Rd, Barking.

We handle everything from spouse and skilled worker visas through to sponsor licence applications, indefinite leave to remain, and British citizenship — and we're proud that our advice is available in English, Urdu, Punjabi, Hindi, Albanian and Turkish, so clients can discuss their case in the language they're most comfortable in.
