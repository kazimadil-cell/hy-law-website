Typical Case Scenarios

Every case is different, so alongside our verified outcomes above, here's how a few other common matters typically unfold with our support.

Illustrative examples, not a specific past client
Skilled Worker Visa

Sponsored employee relocating for a new role

A licensed UK employer issues a Certificate of Sponsorship for a new hire moving from overseas. We prepare the visa application alongside the employer's sponsor duties, from salary and skill-level checks through to the final decision.

Typical Processing~3 Weeks*
See Skilled Worker Visa Services
Indefinite Leave to Remain

Settling permanently after a qualifying period

A client nearing the end of their 5-year (or 10-year long residence) qualifying period applies to settle permanently in the UK. We review their continuous residence and absences, then prepare the ILR application accordingly.

Typical Processing~6 Months*
See Indefinite Leave to Remain Services
British Citizenship

Naturalisation after settlement

Twelve months after being granted ILR, a client becomes eligible to apply for British citizenship. We check their residence and Life in the UK requirements before submitting the naturalisation application.

Typical Processing~4-6 Months*
See British Citizenship Services

*Standard Home Office processing times, correct as of publication — actual timeframes vary by case and can change.
